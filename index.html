About Me

I am a very passionate person, over the past couple of years, I’ve dabbled in many areas of life such as sports, music, and tech to name a few. More recently, however, someone piqued my interest, and that’s you.

Truth be told, I wanted us to get close in a way like friends do from the start. I never really expected my feelings to grow, reaching a point where it’s currently 10:28 PM as I’m making this letter, and I’m also really nervous as I anxiously triple-check every word that I write.

Before I forget to introduce myself,

Hi! It’s me, Kurt. I can only imagine what your reaction will be after reading that. I’m not sure if you’re surprised, but I’m willing to bet that it’s likely that you are. I mean I’m always walking on eggshells when I’m around you. This leads to us having the most awkward conversations ever t_t…

Also hindi rin maayos ung transitions from paragraphs to paragraphs (medyo sabog na, maam cd kasi eh.) I don’t want to force my feelings onto you, I totally understand, and respect if you can’t reciprocate them.

After this letter, I know things will change between us, for better or for worse. I’m risking it all because it’s you, and to me, you’re worth that risk.
